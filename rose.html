Industrial Enclosures

Rose Systemtechnik is a pioneer in industrial enclosure technology. From the conception in 1969 of a standard terminal enclosure for engines and machinery a significant product range development as taken place to include more than 2,000 enclosures.

Industrial enclosures made of aluminium, stainless steel, polycarbonate, polyester and ABS demonstrate a wide range of application possibilities based on their universal design.

They are suitable for installations of mechanical, electrical and electronic components. In addition to the standard design as a terminal box, the product range also offers flange enclosures, electronic profile enclosures, enclosures for membrane keypads and touch installations, cabinets, desktop enclosures, push button stations, and stainless steel enclosures for hygienic applications.

For applications which are not covered by the standard product range or where there is demand for an individual design, there is also an option of developing a customised enclosure – from the initial design phase to the construction, assembly and mounting phase through to the complete wiring and testing phase.

Application range of industrial enclosures: 

  • Communications and MSR technology
  • PC and data technology
  • Medical technology and the pharmaceutical industry
  • Mechanical and plant engineering
  • Automation technology
  • Food and beverage industry

Operating and display enclosures

Ergonomic operator guidance is absolutely necessary for the operation of modern machinery and equipment. Individual modular construction concepts – consisting of control enclosures, suspension systems and high adjustment units – provide operators with optimal system solutions to suit their specific case of application.

Important: In this area, machine controls or touch panels must be adapted to the harsh environmental conditions of the machinery.

ROSE operating and display enclosures provide reliable protection for installed electronic components and offer the best possible user convenience.

SL 2000, SL 3000 and SL 4000 aluminium profile enclosure systems meet the necessary requirements according to size and depth variability. Commander systems can be designed flexibly with maximum dimensions (L x W x D) of 1500 x 1200 x 600 mm.

Customised aluminium suspension systems for the connection between machinery and control unit allow the creation of an individual design based on universal configurations. An ergonomic control concept will be achieved in connection with height adjustment units.

The requirements for stationary systems also apply to mobile operating units. Larger units can be moved from one place to another by Moterm mobile stand systems; manually operated enclosures will be used for smaller systems.

The application range of the operating and display enclosure:

  • Mechanical and plant engineering
  • Automation technology
  • Packaging technology
  • MSR technology
  • All areas where ergonomically operating units are used

Ex Equipment

Special safety guidelines become relevant that prescribe the use of Ex tested components apply to all potentially explosive atmospheres. For this purpose, the ATEX directive applies within the EU and regulates the safe operation of plants and systems in potentially explosive areas. In other countries and continents, other safety guidelines apply in this area (IECEx, TR-TS, NEC, etc.) – often these are derivations of the European ATEX legislation.

In Europe, all these guidelines are summarised in the standard EN 60079 for the electrical components. This defines different protection concepts which – depending on the electrical requirements – will all enable equally safe solutions.

Ex enclosures made of aluminium, polyester and stainless steel as well as control stations and other Ex equipment offer reliable basic protection for electrical distributors used in potentially explosive areas. The application range of the products is categorised in zone 1 and 2 for gas explosion protection, and in zone 21 and 22 for dust explosion protection. For different applications, different protection concepts will be available:

  • Ex-e increased safety
  • Ex-i intrinsic safety
  • Ex-d flameproof enclosure

Typical application ranges include:

  • Chemical and petrochemical industry
  • Process industry
  • Lacquer-processing industry
  • Mining
  • Silo installation
