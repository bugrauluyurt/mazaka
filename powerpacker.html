Motion control solutions in medical sector

For more than twenty years, power-packer actuators for medical applications have proven their quality in furniture of the world’s leading manufacturers. Stable height adjustment of beds, stretchers and tables, as well as quality patient lifts are equipped with our cylinders and pumps. MK5 and Stretcher Actuator are well known actuators and are adapted to customer's requirements.

New applications concern scanner tables, that need accurate, clean and reliable actuations. By introducing these scanner table actuations, Power-Packer’s product portfolio in the medical field is extended with electro hydraulic actuations.

MK-5


The MK5 can be used in the following applications:

  • Hospital beds
  • Home care beds
  • Patient lifting devices
  • Examination couches
  • Physiotherapy tables
  • Chairs
  • Other applications

The MK5 is a reliable self-contained hydraulic actuator, of which more than a million have already been built. The pump, cylinder, valves and reservoir are combined into one, compact, maintenance-free unit.

Every MK5 comes with a pressure relief valve and a flow control valve to ensure smooth descent. The MK5 is easy to install and is built for long life.

MMC


MMC (Manual Multi Cylinders) characteristics:

  • Operate up to 4 cylinders with one foot pump
  • Easy function selection
  • Single and double acting cylinders
  • High forces comfortable to operate
  • 8,5 and 14 kN push forces
  • System tested and pre-filled

The MMC can be used in the following applications:

  • Operating Tables
  • Beds
  • Phychiatric Beds
  • Medical Chairs
  • Stretchers

Electro-Hydraulic MMC


The Electro-Hydraulic MMC is a reliable system designed to operate multiple movements in the most innovative medical applications. It combines the need of an electro-hydraulic and manual-hydraulic system, with up to 4 manual back up functions if power supply fails.

The system enables the user, by means of a selector operated by a handset, to select which cylinder to operate. The selector is integrated in the pump which results in less build-in space and less weight. All available double acting cylinders can be combined, this gives you the flexibility to create the most suitable system for your application.

Quattro


The QUATTRO can be used in the following applications:

  • Scan and operating tables
  • Other applications with need of extremely precise adjustment at variable loads and/or velocities.

The QUATTRO is a newly developed electro-hydraulic actuator for the high end of the market. With this system it is possible to vary load and velocity in either direction independently of each other.

Stretcher Actuator


The Stretcher Actuator is a reliable self-contained hydraulic actuator for use in stretchers and trolleys. The pump, cylinder, valves and reservoir are combined into one compact, maintenance free unit.

The Stretcher Actuator with its integrated guidance can handle high slide loads. Therefore, no additional actuation or support mechanism is needed in the design of the stretcher and trolley. This can lower the manufacturing cost of the stretcher and trolley. The Stretcher Actuator is easy to install and is built for long life.

The stretcher actuator can be used in the following applications:

  • Stretchers
  • Emergency Trolleys
  • Hospital Beds
  • Other applications

DMI


The DMI is a reliable hydraulic actuator set for every application where our self-contained units cannot solve your problem. Pump, cylinder, couplers and hoses are combined into one, compact, maintenance-free unit.

Pump and cylinder are connected through hoses. The set is delivered fully assembled and tested. The DMI is ideal for applications with limited space.

Every DMI comes with a pressure relief valve and a flow control valve to ensure smooth descent. The DMI is easy to install and is built for long life.

The DMI can be used in the following applications:

  • Hospital beds
  • Stretchers
  • Emergency trolleys
  • Operation tables
  • Other applications

Medipack 5100

Every MEDIPACK 5100 comes with a standard flow control valve to ensure smooth descent. The MEDIPACK 5100 is easy to install and is built for long life.
