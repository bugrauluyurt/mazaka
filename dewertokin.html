Dewert - Hospital/Care

DEWERT develops, manufactures and markets electromotive drives, control units and operating elements for ergonomic applications in medical devices.

In this sector, DEWERT occupies a leading position in many markets and individual segments. The long-term goal is to ensure continued expansion, and this can only be done by permanent innovation and a high level of competitiveness as regards costs, quality and service.

Okin - Office

In office situations, for a long time the aim was to facilitate the employees' comfort and to offer them a work environment which was as comfortable as possible and in which devices including the telephone, printer and photocopier could be accessed from a sitting position.

The OKIN Deskdrive lifting columns and Office single drives offer you the perfect solution for the flexible assembly of reliable modular components.

Okin Seating

As a global market leader for electromotor-driven seating actuators, OKIN offers pioneering drive systems within an extensive product range of single actuators and double motors, including a comprehensive range of accessories and versions.

The thoughtful design and employment of microprocessor technology allows ever-more intelligent and sophisticated controls, allowing for diverse applications in many areas. Even simple products as the ergonomically designed handset CURVELINE or the power-saving switch mode power supply technology (SMPS) round out our portfolio.
